Moscow Mule

Moscow Mule Recipe:

The Moscow Mule is a classic cocktail made with vodka, ginger beer, and lime juice, typically served in a copper mug. To prepare, fill a copper mug with ice cubes, add vodka, and squeeze fresh lime juice over the ice. Top it off with ginger beer and stir gently to combine. Garnish with a lime wedge or wheel and a sprig of fresh mint. The Moscow Mule is known for its refreshing and zesty flavor, making it a popular choice for summer gatherings and parties.

For a twist on the traditional recipe, try using flavored vodka such as citrus, berry, or ginger-infused varieties. You can also experiment with different types of ginger beer to find your preferred level of sweetness and spiciness. Some variations include muddling fresh fruit or herbs in the cocktail for added complexity. Whether enjoyed on a hot day or as a festive drink during the holidays, the Moscow Mule is a versatile and easy-to-make cocktail that is sure to impress your guests.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz (60 ml) vodka
  • 1/2 oz (15 ml) fresh lime juice
  • 4 oz (120 ml) ginger beer
  • Lime wedge or wheel, for garnish
  • Fresh mint sprig, for garnish

Steps:

  1. Fill a copper mug with ice cubes.
  2. Add vodka and fresh lime juice.
  3. Top with ginger beer and stir gently.
  4. Garnish with a lime wedge or wheel and a sprig of fresh mint.
